places must visit

Starbucks Reserve Roastery

I had visited November 22nd, 2022

The starbucks reserve roastery is one of the biggest starbucks stores in the USA. It is filled with modern lights, technology, and amazing designs. This starbucks sells all sorts of food, even ones you may not find at your regular daily starbucks. For example, Tiramisu. I've seen the first 2 floors and they're so pretty. I took many photos and would recommend people visiting while in Chicago.



Athena Greek Restaurant

I went on December 3rd, 2022

Athena is a big, beautiful restaurant. I saw many greek mythology related statues and artwork around the store. Including nice lights and amazing food. Some orders were even made infront of you, with fire. Me and my friends enjoyed eating there and it would be a good place to come to if you like Greek Food.



Brooklyn Boulders

I went on December 3rd, 2022

Brooklyn Boulders is a bouldering, rock climbing, and even gym area. Members can go and rent shoes and harnesses for bouldering & toproping. I saw many different difficulties for bouldering and attempted many as well. The members switch out the areas every week or so to switch difficulties and get people to experience something new. Definitely a nice spot for people into being active.



Ranalli's

I went on November 5th, 2022

Ranalli's is a pizzeria in the West Loop. I enjoyed their food and the way their restaurant is organized. It's good quality and I would definitely eat there again. I would recommend going if you're craving pizza.



Six Flags: Great America

I went on June 7th, 2022

Six flags is one of the most famous amusement parks in the USA. I went on the Dark Knight ride which is an indoor rollercoaster. It has 2 big drops and a few sharp turns. Six Flags is a fun and popular place that my school went to together. I'd definitely recommend going, especially with friends.

review on the movies

Nightmare Before Christmas
Amazing film for kids

Watched a few weeks ago with many class/schoolmates. It is a movie about the director of every halloween feeling trapped in this world of his. Feeling as if he wants more, and that the years he had directed halloween were getting boring for him. Eventually, he finds something new. It didn't go as planned though, leading to the points up till the end of the movie. A very nice show to watch in groups, would recommend.

Dora and the Lost City Of Gold
Very interesting movie; good lesson

Watched when the movie came out. It is a movie about Dora, who had lived out in the wild for years, had finally been sent to a public high school. Eventually, she has to solve a mystery of a lost civilization to save her parents. This movie was amazing and definitely was stuck in my head for a few weeks afterward. Would recommend.

Minions: The Rise of Gru
Fun movie

Watched with some friends not too long ago. A very fun and dramatic movie. Young Gru looks up to a group of Villians, "Vicious 6", and his idol ends up getting kicked out. Seeking for revenge, he tries to join the Vicious 6 and they end up getting lots of tension between them, while they both fight for a sacred, dangerous item. It's a great movie for young children and definitely gave me a few giggles.

Encanto
Inspiring

A very nice and popular movie for kids. It is about a family who has had these gifts (sort of powers) passed down throughout the years. One generation, Mirabel (main character) doesn't get a gift and is treated unfairly. The end of the movie is shocking & sad and definitely can teach a nice lesson to kids. Would recommend. (also has catchy songs).
